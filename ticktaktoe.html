To play Tic-Tac-Toe, two players take turns marking spaces in a 3×3 grid—one uses X and the other uses O. The goal is to be the first to get three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty square, and the game ends when one player wins or when all squares are filled with no winner, resulting in a draw
